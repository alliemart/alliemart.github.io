About

Texas A&M '16 | 12th Man Productions Alum | Texas Charge

My name is Alexandra, but my family and friends like to call me Allie. I graduated from Texas A&M University with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science and an Art minor. During my time in college, I was a student worker for 12th Man Productions. I had the opportunity to make videos and graphics needed for Texas A&M Athletics. That included producing big screen shows and operating cameras for SEC Network broadcasts. For two seasons, I was the Aggie Softball team videographer. I created pump up videos and highlight features for the team throughout the season. My game footage was also used for 12th Man Production's TV show titled "The Beat".

Right now, I am the Media Director for the Texas Charge which is a National Pro Fastpitch team. I create media content for the team which can range from photos, video, and graphics and I maintain all of our social media platforms. I have a huge passion for the game of softball and I strive to grow the game anyway that I can.

Services

Here are a few services that I offer. If something in particular is not listed below that you need, feel free to ask and I'll see what I can do!
Recruiting Videos
Want to play college ball, but need a recruiting video to send to coaches? Well you're in luck because I'd be more than happy to help you out! Not only do you get a high quality video, but you also get a webpage on this website that you can also show off to coaches. For more information and previous recruiting videos: Click Here
Game Footage
Game footage is everything. Not only can you see your mechanics, but you can also use the footage I capture to send to college coaches. I happen to have two seasons of experience shooting game footage for Texas A&M softball, so you can count on me to never miss a play.
Promo Videos
Promo videos have a special way of stirring emotions, meaning, they can have a huge impact on people. That's why I love creating and editing these kind of videos any chance I get. Every frame can make a difference.
Game Photos
A picture is worth a thousand words. So cliche, but so true! I can definitely help you get those cool action shots during a couple of your games.
Graphic Design
Whether you need a logo or promotional graphics, I got you. Expect to see clean and simple designs!
Web Design
We all know a clean and simple website is the way to someone's heart. I can't promise you'll get a date with the person you're trying to impress, but I can promise you a website that will catch people's attention.
Web Development
The cool thing about creating websites is that you have an opportunity to give people or a company exposure, which is why I try my best to make my websites enjoyable and easy to navigate for all users.
Troubleshooting
Technology is a blessing, but it can also be a pain. Don't worry though, I can help get rid of that annoying virus and those wifi complications you've been having for months.
