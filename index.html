about

My name is Alexandra, but my family and friends like to call me Allie. I'm a junior Computer Science major at Texas A&M University who has a passion for graphics and software. I tend to be more technical than artistic, but my Art minor has really been helping me get on my creative side. I also love editing videos. I currently work at 12th Man Productions as a student worker, so I get to make videos and graphics needed for Texas A&M Athletics. That includes big screen shows and SEC Network broadcasts. As of right now, I am working as a videographer for the Aggie Softball team. My co-worker and I make pump up videos and features for the team any chance we get. Our game footage is also used for 12th Man Production's TV show titled "The Beat".

  • design

    I'm a huge fan of minimalist art. I love simplicity, so I take that approach when I create graphics,software, or videos.

  • app developement

    The cool thing about creating apps is that you have an opportunity to make people's lives easier, which is why I try my best to make my apps enjoyable and easy to use for all users.

  • videos

    Videos have a special way of stirring emotions, meaning, they can have a huge impact on people. That's why I love creating and editing videos any chance I get. Every frame can make a difference.
