Description

  • Client: Dallas Charge
  • Date: May - August 2016

The Dallas Charge is a women's professional softball based out of Dallas, Texas. The team joined the National Pro Fastpitch league in the summer of 2015. The Charge provides elite softball players the opportunity to pursue a professional career and inspire the next generation of professionals.

In May 2016, I joined the Dallas Charge as their Media Director. I create media content for the team which can range from photos, video, and graphics and I maintain all of our social media platforms.These are a few photos that I took during my first season with the Charge to help promote our team. A few of my pictures have been used by Wilson, DeMarini, and Combat.
