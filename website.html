Interactive Information Website
A website that was created to let users learn about Texas A&M Softball.

Description

  • Client: CSCE 444 | Interactive Information
  • Date: May 7, 2016
  • Online: Texas A&M Softball Project

My senior year of college, I ended up taking a Interactive Information class for my Computer Science degree. The point of the final project was to create an interactive website for users. We had to come up with a concept behind our website. I decided to use the concept of syncronization. Whenever elements are syncronized, the result can make a major impact on a user. For example, a video. When video is syncronized with music, the product is capable of stirring emotions for an individual. Therefore, for this website I let users interact with information from the Texas A&M Softball team while watching a video related to the content they are looking viewing. I created four different sections of information for users.

Pump Up | View the team roster and upcoming schedule.
Legacy | Learn about Texas A&M's history.
Players | Get to know the players through social media.
Seniors | View the 2015 senior's accomplishments.
