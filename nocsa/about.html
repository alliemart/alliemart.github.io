Vision Statement

Mission Statement

Our mission is to promote the manufacturing and installation of quality one-coat stucco through education to NOCSA members, building departments, architects and specifiers, builders, lath and plaster contractors and the public; to meet and exceed the standards that are set for the industry.

Membership

  • Recognition through NOCSA promotion.
  • Association and communication with regular and associate members.
  • Member referral to architects, home builders, general contractors and owners.
  • Industry representation to the national bodies - ICBO, SBCCI, BOCA and ICC.
  • Annual membership directory.
  • Annual membership directory.

Promotion

  • NOCSA promotional brochure for one coat stucco that you can use to promote your product and the industry.
  • NOCSA website.
  • Seminars, workshops and trade show participation with builder, specifier, and trade contractor groups.
  • Support with local building departments and building inspectors as an industry voice for your business.
  • Articles in industry publications.

Technical/Education

  • Current code requirements
  • Specifications
  • Industry Standards
  • New products and methodology
  • Workmanship and quality
  • Legal obligations
