The Texas Charge is a women's professional softball based out of New Braunfels, Texas. The team joined the National Pro Fastpitch league in the summer of 2015, formerly known as the Dallas Charge. The Charge provides elite softball players the opportunity to pursue a professional career and inspire the next generation of professionals.

In May 2016, I joined the Charge as their Media Director. I create media content for the team which can range from photos, video, and graphics and I maintain all of our social media platforms. These are a few graphics that I made using Photoshop for my first season with the Charge to help promote our team.
